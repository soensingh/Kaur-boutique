Tailoring & Alteration

we pride ourselves on our high end style of tailoring. We offer alterations for both men and women in formal traditional garments as well as casual wear, specialized in women.

Made-to-Measure

Made-to-measure is a custom based on our exclusive fitting block, also known as our try-on. With this try-on, we use 35 distinct measurements to ensure you are designing a garment with a remarkable fit!

Made-to-Order

This is our small batch production made just for you. We give you a range of custom design options for a specific garment (colors, buttons, style, etc.) and, once ordered, we begin working on it immediately.
